Why This Exists

One enterprise assessment instead of a fragmented questionnaire.

The uploaded methodology and manual are aligned around one management question: can each market systematically convert RGM opportunity into sustained value creation?

  • Link market assessments to strategic ambition and profitable growth.
  • Separate business performance diagnosis from capability maturity diagnosis.
  • Expose where the real bottleneck sits: insight, strategy, policy, execution, or sustainability.
  • Create a repeatable operating cycle for reassessment, calibration, and action planning.

Operating Cycle

Designed to become a recurring management system, not a one-off survey.

1 Fact base and pre-read

Build the market context and prepare evidence before self-scoring begins.

2 Market self-assessment

Score the detailed pillar questions with evidence-backed maturity anchors.

3 Interview and validation

Test whether the self-scores match actual decisions, routines, and governance quality.

4 Calibration

Moderate scores, align evidence standards, and confirm the final maturity picture.

5 Report and roadmap

Translate the dashboard into priorities, ownership, and an action roadmap.

6 Pulse and follow-up

Use lighter annual reviews and quarterly tracking to keep progress visible between full cycles.

Backend Pattern

GitHub Pages in front, Google Apps Script in the middle, Google Sheets behind.

1. GitHub Pages

Hosts the static site, methodology pages, questionnaire logic, and instant result dashboard.

2. Apps Script web app

Receives a submission payload from the browser and appends the result set into Sheets.

3. Google Sheets

Acts as a lightweight admin console for sessions, question-level responses, and priority outputs.

Recommended sheet tabs

  • Assessment_Sessions: one row per completed assessment with summary metrics.
  • Assessment_Responses: one row per question response with score, evidence, and comment.
  • Assessment_Priority: top priorities for each submitted session.
